### Root Cause Analysis

**103 pages (97% of all not-indexed) are "crawled - currently not indexed."** This is the blog's defining SEO problem.

Google's crawler visits these 103 pages but decides they don't deserve a spot in the index. This happens when:

1. **No domain authority** — blog.plantdoctor.app has **ZERO external backlinks** from third-party sites. Google has no trust signals to warrant indexing content from an unknown domain.
2. **Content quality threshold** — Without authority, content must be exceptionally unique and comprehensive to earn indexing. Blog posts may be hitting a quality threshold.
3. **Competitive topics** — Queries like "neem oil safe on edible plants" and "calathea leaves curling" have established authority sites (thespruce.com, gardeningknowhow.com) already ranking. Google sees no reason to index yet another answer from an unknown blog.

**The fix is clear: build backlinks.** With even 10-20 quality backlinks, many of these 103 pages would likely move from "crawled not indexed" to "indexed" — Google just needs a trust signal.
